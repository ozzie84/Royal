Serving Los Angeles

Three best friends who worked together in the moving business for over six years had a dream. They wanted to start a company of their own, so they established Royal Moving in 2012. Why start a Los Angeles moving company? They had a passion for helping people, and with their six years of experience they knew the business and were good at it. Royal Moving has built a team of movers with the same motivation, standards and dedication to customer service as the three founders.
